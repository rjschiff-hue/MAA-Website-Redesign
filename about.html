Our Story

A National Platform Built Over Decades

Multifamily Asset Advisors was built on a simple premise: investors deserve advisors who have navigated the same challenges, risks, and opportunities they face. Our team brings decades of hands-on experience acquiring, managing, and optimizing multifamily portfolios across the United States.

With over $350 million in assets under management and ownership, we have the scale and sophistication of an institutional firm, combined with the personalized attention of a boutique advisory practice.

We work alongside our clients at every stage of the investment lifecycle — from initial acquisition through active management and strategic disposition — ensuring every decision is made with precision and purpose.

The People

Meet Your Advisors

Each member of our team brings a unique background and deep specialization in multifamily real estate.

WH
Founder & Managing Partner

Wes Hill

Wes Hill began his property management career while attending the University of San Diego in the late 1980s, where he took on responsibilities including lawn care, painting apartments, and bookkeeping — learning the multifamily business from the ground up.

That hands-on foundation has informed Wes's approach to every aspect of the business. Over more than three decades, he has built a national platform with a reputation for operational excellence, disciplined underwriting, and strong investor relationships.

Today, Wes leads Multifamily Asset Advisors and oversees a portfolio of high-quality assets exceeding $350 million under management and ownership. He is a sought-after speaker and commentator on multifamily investing, having appeared on podcasts and industry panels including the Real Estate Espresso Podcast.

Asset Management Acquisitions Strategy
TE
Senior Advisor

Tim Edwards

A native of Texas, Tim Edwards started his professional career in 1979 in Sacramento, California, launching more than four decades of expertise in multifamily brokerage and investment advisory.

Tim established himself as a consistent top producer at Marcus & Millichap in the North State region for more than five years, building deep relationships with buyers, sellers, and investors across Northern California and beyond.

His extensive brokerage background gives him unparalleled market insight into pricing dynamics, deal flow, and transaction execution — skills he brings to every client engagement at Multifamily Asset Advisors.

Brokerage Dispositions Market Analysis
PB
Advisor

Pat Bloom

Pat Bloom holds a graduate degree from the prestigious University of Chicago and brings 30 years of small business experience in hospitality, sales, and real estate across diverse markets including upstate New York, Phoenix, and Northern California.

This breadth of experience gives Pat a distinctive perspective on operations, customer experience, and the business fundamentals that drive sustainable value in multifamily real estate assets.

Pat's analytical rigor — shaped by his University of Chicago training — combined with his practical entrepreneurial experience makes him a valuable advisor on operational improvements, underwriting, and risk assessment.

Operations Due Diligence Risk Assessment
RS
Advisor

Randy Schiff

Randy Schiff has been a saver and investor since he was 18 years old, developing an early and enduring passion for building wealth through disciplined financial management and real estate investment.

In 1995, with $6,000 cash and a loan from a friend, he bought and renovated his first multifamily property. Over the next 25 years, he owner-operated the portfolio while progressively consolidating much of it into larger holdings.

Randy has been involved in over $175 million of transactions as principal, built 22 homes, and renovated hundreds of apartments and houses. He now has ownership in a $100 million portfolio with properties located in California, Oregon, Idaho, Nevada and Iowa.

With his construction experience, Randy adds operational expertise to budgeting and project Management. For his clients, he brings a capital markets perspective to multifamily investing, helping them create more passive portfolios and build long-term investment strategies aligned with their goals.
